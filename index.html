Styling

H1: Main page heading

  • Font-weight: 700 (bold)
  • Font-size: 26px
  • Font-family: Roboto Condensed

H2: Subheading

  • Font-weight: 600 (semibold)
  • Font-size: 18px
  • Font-family: Noto Sans

P: Paragraph text

  • Font-weight: 400 (regular)
  • Font-size: 14px
  • Font-family: Barlow
